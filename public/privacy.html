Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

Silicon collects no data whatsoever. The app runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. There is no account, no login, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

What data do we collect?

None. Zero. We do not collect any personal information, device identifiers, usage data, crash reports, location data, or any other information from you.

Where is my game data stored?

Your save game is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage on web; localStorage via Capacitor on iOS). It never leaves your device and is not synced to any cloud service.

Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser's site data will erase the save. Use the Export save option in Settings to create a backup.

Third-party SDKs and services

None. The app contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporters, or social login libraries. The only third-party code is Capacitor (for native iOS packaging) and Apple's StoreKit (for the optional in-app purchase). Neither transmits your personal data to us.

In-app purchases

The app offers one optional non-consumable in-app purchase: Creative Mode ($2.99). This purchase is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase records are held by Apple per their Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Silicon does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. Since we collect no data, any changes would be purely clarificatory.
